THE X-MEN

The X-Men are a super hero team comprised of mutants. They've had multiple different iterations of the team throughout the years. They have expanded their roster since their first introduction and at times have multiple teams at once. Charles Xavier "Professor X" created the team to help the world and mutants. Scott Summers "Cyclops" is the original X-men member and Leader
